# Sample audio provenance

These 7 clips are extracted from the **LibriSpeech ASR corpus**
(`dev-clean` subset), used here to seed the app with real, varied speech on
first run instead of a single repeated synthetic sentence.

[CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). Source:
<https://www.openslr.org/12/>. Audio derived from LibriVox recordings of
public-domain texts.

Converted from the corpus's original FLAC to 16kHz mono WAV; otherwise
unmodified.
